{{#_}}Project Idea Guidelines{{/_}}

Community members can generate initial project ideas at Neighborhood Assembly meetings and "pop-up" Participatory Budgeting events across town.

Eligible projects must:

  • {{#_}}Be identified within a City Council Ward.{{/_}}
  • {{#_}}Be on City-owned property (not on other government-owned property such as schools).{{/_}}
  • {{#_}}Be a capital improvement (something that can be bought, built, or repaired).{{/_}}
