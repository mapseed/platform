{{#_}}Greensboro Participatory Budgeting:{{/_}}

{{#_}}Our Community, Our Decision!{{/_}}


{{#_}}Greensboro Participatory Budgeting is a democratic process in which community members from all five City Council districts are directly deciding how to spend $500,000 ($100,000 per district).{{/_}}

{{#_}}From April to November 2017, volunteer budget delegates will work to develop these initial ideas into full project proposals. Greensboro residents will able to vote in October 2017 to determine the winning projects. {{/_}}

{{#_}}For more information on participatory budgeting in Greensboro, visit greensboro-nc.gov/pb.{{/_}}

{{#_}}For information on the nationwide movement for PB, visit the website of the Participatory Budgeting Project{{/_}}.
