Hello! You're looking at a demo of {{ config.app.name }}, a mapping project from OpenPlans.

Shareabouts is a simple web app for crowdsourced mapping. You can use it to collect public input on maps - for example, where should a new park be built? Which bus stops need a new shelter? People can put dots on the map, add comments, discuss other people's suggestions and share locations over social networks.

In this demo here, we're collecting info on good places to eat near our office. But you could use it for anything.

Read more about Shareabouts at shareabouts.org, or head to Shareabouts on github. The project is open source, and we welcome your involvement. You can run Shareabouts on DotCloud, Heroku, and similar.
